About me

Hi, I'm Will, a final year DPhil student based in the Nuffield Department of Medicine, University of Oxford, UK. My research sits within computational biology, where I am interested in how antibiotic resistance spreads between bacteria. More technically, I work on developing new genomic tools for studying mobile antimicrobial resistance genes, such as those on plasmids, transposons, or integrons.
